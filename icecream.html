Ice Cream

Description

Ice cream is a sweet and frozen dessert loved by everyone. It is creamy, delicious, and perfect for hot days.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

Steps

  1. Mix milk, cream, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Stir well until sugar dissolves.
  3. Add vanilla essence and mix.
  4. Pour into a container and freeze for 6-8 hours.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!
