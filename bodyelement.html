On a quiet evening, a young boy named Arun found a small wooden box near the river. Curious, he opened it and discovered a glowing stone inside. When he touched it, visions of distant worlds filled his mind. Frightened yet amazed, he ran home and told his grandmother. She smiled gently and said it was a gift meant for brave hearts. From that day, Arun carried hope wherever he went, believing magic lived within him always, and that the smallest discovery could change a life forever. He grew into a kind and fearless explorer, inspiring others to dream beyond limits.
